What we do

At Save The Seas, we are dedicated to cleaning and protecting seas, oceans, and beaches.

Our team of volunteers works globally to remove plastic, waste, and harmful debris from coastlines and marine environments. We organize regular beach clean-ups, promote sustainable practices, and collaborate with local communities to raise awareness about ocean pollution. By actively reducing waste and restoring ecosystems, we aim to protect marine life and preserve natural habitats for future generations. Our mission is simple: a cleaner, healthier planet, free from ocean pollution
